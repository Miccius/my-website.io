About Me

A Short Story

My journey into web development is a tale of curiosity and reinvention. I've worn many hats - from navigating the bustling world of hospitality to troubleshooting in manufacturing. Each role taught me something invaluable, but it was the hum of technology that truly captivated me.
One day, while juggling customer needs and operational challenges, I realized my true calling lay in the digital realm. Armed with determination and fueled by late-night coding sessions, I embarked on a self-taught adventure through the landscapes of web Developement stacks. The thrill of bringing ideas to life on screen was intoxicating.
My passion led me to self-studing and Makers Academy, where I honed my skills and transformed from an eager novice to a capable developer. Now, I blend my diverse background with technical expertise to create web experiences that are not just functional, but truly resonate with users. Every project is a new story waiting to be told, and I'm here to craft it, line by line, pixel by pixel.
